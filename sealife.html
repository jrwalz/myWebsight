Ecosystem of the American Northwest Coast

There are many unique species with some varieties that may be hard to find elsewhere. Some of the longest lived and largest in size dwell in and around these waters and coastal areas. Many of the species are either in or have been in the endangered species list. When I was growing up all of the talk was about how great the Pacific Northwest is and now everything seems to be about how much it is in danger to climate change and the influence of human beings.
